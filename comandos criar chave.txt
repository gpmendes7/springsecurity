dentro da pasta resources:

 gera chave 
 openssl genrsa > aux.key

 converte chave para formato pkcs#8
 openssl pkcs8 -topk8 -inform pem -in aux.key -outform pem -nocrypt -out app.key

 gera chave publica
 openssl rsa -in app.key -pubout -out app.pub
